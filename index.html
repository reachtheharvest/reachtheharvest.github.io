Do you have coconuts, mangos, avocados, or any fruit dropping and littering the yard?

If you do not want to deal with rotting fruit littering your yard, call us at (808)747-9447 or click here to schedule a harvest. If you are having trouble with the scheduler read this tutorial.

About

Matt Titchenal

Coconut Climber

I have been climbing coconut trees on the Big Island since 2010. I have climbed to harvest coconuts and climbed to stop the tree from producing coconuts. I love coconut water so I prefer to harvest the coconuts! I care about the trees that I climb and take care to do a quality job. I have worked on farms in Hawaii for many years learning about how to harvest and maintain many of the fruit trees that grow here.

Services

I have climbed hundreds of trees and have a great appreciation and respect for trees, so I will give detailed attention to the tree and its surroundings. To come back on a regular basis is what will make the care of the tree profitable. I will set up the regular scheduling and you can earn passive income that is annually after it is requested

No-Cost Profit Sharing

  • Free Regulary Scheduled Tree harvesting and pruning
  • Profit sharing with the money made from the harvest
  • Yard is cleaned up and waste is used to make a compost pile
  • Coupons to buy other fruit harvested at the farmers market
  • Unused fruit is donated to a food bank
  • Some trees may be too large to harvest
